Current Events

The Finland Chamber of Commerce is actively engaging the business community with a series of impactful events. Recently, they hosted a business breakfast with the Council of American States in Europe, where Finnish companies explored opportunities for U.S. expansion. Additionally, they organized a webinar on navigating Irish markets, providing valuable insights for businesses looking to expand into Ireland. These events are part of their ongoing efforts to support international trade and provide networking opportunities for local businesses.

Monthly Spotlights

Nokia

Nokia, a global leader in telecommunications, continues to revolutionize the industry with its cutting-edge technology and innovative solutions. With a rich history spanning over 150 years, Nokia has evolved from a humble paper mill to a tech giant at the forefront of 5G networks, IoT, and advanced mobile devices. Their commitment to sustainability and digital transformation has positioned them as a key player in connecting people and businesses worldwide. Through strategic partnerships and relentless innovation, Nokia is shaping the future of communication and driving the digital age forward.

Kone

Founded over a century ago, Kone has become synonymous with reliability, safety, and cutting-edge design.
